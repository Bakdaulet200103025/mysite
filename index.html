Third Stage

Fourth Stage

THE BEST WORKS

Оur best projects are all work done in high-tech design.To many, the design of a high-tech apartment seems overly strict, dry, even lifeless.But it is on the basis of this strict direction that it is possible to create original and cozy interiors.

High-tech design is minimalism, ergonomics, modern building materials and advanced technologies, light and space.Distinctive features are the lack of decor and the dominance of functionality. High-tech in the interior of the apartment focuses on the shape and texture of architectural details, objects, leaving in the background colors and patterns. From materials, it makes it possible to freely experiment with combinations of glass, metal and plastic.
